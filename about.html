Our Story

Passion, tradition, and culinary excellence.

Who We Are

Welcome to North East Halal Catering, where our passion for authentic, high-quality halal cuisine meets our dedication to exceptional service. Founded on the principles of family, community, and tradition, we have been serving the North East with pride for over a decade.

Our Vision

Our vision is simple: to be the most trusted and celebrated halal caterer in the region. We believe that food is more than just sustenance; it's a way to bring people together. We are committed to using only the finest, freshest, and 100% certified halal ingredients in all our dishes.

Meet Our Head Chef

Chef Ibrahim Ahmed

Executive Chef & Founder

With 25 years of culinary experience spanning from Lahore to London, Chef Ibrahim is the heart of our kitchen. He specializes in traditional Punjabi and Mughal recipes, infusing every dish with authentic flavours passed down through generations. His commitment to quality is what sets our catering apart.
